Ingredients:
3 cups cooked, rinsed and drained chickpeas ((2) 15 oz canned chickpeas)
2  limes, squeezed
1/3 cup tahini
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
1/4 cup Sriracha
1/2 cup water

Instructions:
Add all the ingredients to a food processor and begin to process. Add water as needed to produce a smooth consistency. I added about 1/2 cup. Scrape down the sides if needed. Adjust seasonings to taste. Serve with cut up vegetables, pita chips, tortilla chips or enjoy on a sandwich or wrap.&nbsp;
