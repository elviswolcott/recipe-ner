Ingredients:
1/2  green bell pepper thinly sliced
1/2  red bell pepper thinly sliced
1/3  onion thinly sliced
4 cloves of garlic chopped
1/2 cup spinach chopped ((thawed if frozen))
1  medium tomato chopped
1/4 teaspoon chili flakes
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt or to taste
1 Tablespoon garlic infused olive oil
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
2 cups farfalle or any pasta
1/2 cup mexican mix cheese shredded
1/2 teaspoon cajun spice
1 teaspoon lime juice

Instructions:
Boil the farfalle with water and 3/4 teaspoon salt and keep aside when ready.
In a large pan, add vegetable oil and onions and cook on medium heat until onions are translucent.
Add the bell peppers and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the peppers start to soften up.
Add in the spinach and olive oil and cook on medium heat stirring frequently for 2 minutes.
Add in the tomatoes, 1/4 teaspoon salt, black pepper, chili flakes, cajun spice and lemon juice and give it a toss for a few seconds.
Add in the cooked Farfalle or any pasta and toss it well and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
Serve hot topped with the mexican cheese and sprinkled cajun spice!
