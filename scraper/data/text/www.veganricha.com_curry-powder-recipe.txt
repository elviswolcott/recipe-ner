Ingredients:
1  Recipe Garam masala powder
4 tbsp of My Whole Garam masala,  (powdered)
1.5 tsp turmeric
1 tsp ground mustard
3/4 tsp ground ginger (, optional)
1/4 to 1/2 tsp fenugreek seed powder (, optional)
1/2 tsp cayenne (, optional)

Instructions:
Mix all the ingredients and store in an airtight container for upto 3 months.&nbsp;Adjust flavor once you use it with extra cinnamon, heat or turmeric&nbsp;
