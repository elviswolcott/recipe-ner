Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
7 cups canned tomatoes
3  cloves of garlic, minced
1  onion, chopped
1 cup basil
1/2 cup cashews
3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
salt
black pepper
red pepper flakes
more basil to serve
vegan cashew or soy cream

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil and saut√© the onion and the garlic for about 2-3 minutes. Then add the tomatoes and the basil. Cook for a minute. Transfer to a blender, add the cashews and the nutritional yeast, and blend until smooth.
Season the soup with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes and cook for about 3 minutes. Top it with cashew or soy cream, more chopped basil, and red pepper flakes. Serve with fresh bread or vegan cheese sandwiches.
