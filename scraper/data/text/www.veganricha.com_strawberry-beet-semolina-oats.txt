Ingredients:
2  strawberries
half an inch cube of of beet (1 tablespoon chopped beet)
2 tablespoons raw granulated turbinado sugar
1 teaspoon vegan butter
6-7  raw cashews broken into pieces
2 tablespoons raisins
3 tablespoons Semolina (Rava)
2 tablespoons Oats
1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
2-3 tablespoons water or almond milk

Instructions:
Blend and puree the Strawberries and beet with sugar and a teaspoon or 2 of water till nicely pureed.
Add in the oats and blend again to get a thick paste.
In a pan, add the butter and heat on medium low heat till it melts.
Add in the cashews and raisins and cook for 2 minutes till cashews start to turn golden.
Add in the semolina in the pan and roast for 3-4 minutes.
Add in the cardamom powder and strawberry beet and oats puree and mix well stirring continuously.
If the mixture doesnt come together, then add a little water or almond milk.
Take off the heat after a minute and let it sit and cool for 5 minutes.
Shape the mixture into small spheres and serve! I got about 8 small inch rounds!
Store in airtight container in the refrigerator for a few days.
