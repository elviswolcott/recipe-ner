Ingredients:
2  ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup quick oats
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tablespoon natural peanut butter (you can also use any other nut butter such as almond or cashew butter)

Instructions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients with a spoon.
Preheat the oven to 350 Â°F. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and use a spoon to form 12 cookies (each portion should be about 1 1/2 tablespoons of the banana oat mixture).
Bake the cookies for 12 minutes.
