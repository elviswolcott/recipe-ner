Ingredients:
cup all purpose flour
2 cups whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons sugar
1/3 cup honey (add 1 cup if you want a sweet bread)
1/2 cup old fashioned oats cooked in 1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup candied papaya (mango and pineapple)
2 teaspoons oil
1/2 cup water

Instructions:
Warm the water and add sugar and yeast to it. Mix well and keep aside for 10 minutes till it starts getting some bubbles.
In a large pan, mix the all purpose flour, salt and nutmeg.
Add the yeast mixture and mix well.
Cook the oats in milk and let sit till they cool down to a warm state.
Add oats to the flour mix.
Add whole wheat flour one cup at a time to make into a soft smooth dough.
Place dough in a well oiled container. Cover it and place in warm oven for an hour or till it doubles.
Knead dough on a floured surface and flatten into a thick rectangle.
Sprinkle candied papaya, pineapple and mango on it. Roll it up and knead again.
Place dough in a bread pan lightly covered with a wet towel in a warm oven for half an hour.
Remove towel and bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes.
Cool on rack before slicing!
