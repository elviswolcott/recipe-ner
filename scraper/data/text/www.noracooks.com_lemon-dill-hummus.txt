Ingredients:
(1) 15-ounce can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
3 tablespoons tahini
1/4 teaspoon turmeric (for color)
1/2 teaspoon salt
2-3 tablespoons water, more if needed to reach desired consistency

Instructions:
Add the first 6 ingredients to a food processor, and blend until smooth. Add in the water, until the desired creamy consistency is reached.&nbsp;
Garnish with fresh dill if desired, and serve with pita chips, pita bread or various cut vegetables.
