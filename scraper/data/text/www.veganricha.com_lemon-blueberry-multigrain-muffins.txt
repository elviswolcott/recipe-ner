Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole wheat pastry flour
2/3 cup self rising flour (or all purpose flour + 1/4 teaspoon baking powder)
1 tablespoon flax seed meal
3 tablespoons Oats
1 tablespoon cooked quinoa ((optional))
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
Zest of 1 medium lemon
Juice of 1 lemon
1.5 tablespoon high fat oil ((I used organic canola))
1.5 tablespoon roasted Cashew butter ((I used store bought,Homemade nut butter recipes))
5 tablespoons raw granulated sugar or equivalent jaggery or other sweetener. (a couple of tbsps more for a sweeter muffin)
1/4 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup warm water
1/2 cup fresh blueberries
Crumb:
2 tablespoons oats
1 tablespoon coarsely ground oats
1 teaspoon oil
1 teaspoon raw sugar
1 tablespoon self rising flour

Instructions:
In a large bowl, add flax meal, lemon juice, sugar, oil, cashew butter and 1/4 cup (60ml) almost hot water.
Mix it well till the mixture is well combined and a little light. Add in the oats, 1/4 cup (60ml) more water, cooked quinoa(optional, I am on quinoa roll), lemon zest, mix well and keep aside for a few minutes.
In a bowl, mix the flours, salt, baking powder.
Add in the wet mixture and mix for only a few strokes. If the mixture stays too stiff, add more water. do not overmix.
Take 2 Tablespoons of batter. Add 2 Tablespoons oats, coarsely ground Oats, 1 teaspoon of oil , 1 teaspoon raw sugar and 1 tablespoon self rising flour, Mix and keep this for crumble on top. The crumble is optional.
Drop batter in greased muffin pan or muffin cups.
Drop 4-5 blueberries on top and drop some of the crumble.
Bake in preheated oven at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes, until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Let muffins sit for 5 minutes, then take them out and let cool on wire rack. Store in a cool dry box for 2-3 days.
