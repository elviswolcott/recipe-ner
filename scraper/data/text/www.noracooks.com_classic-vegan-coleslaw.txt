Ingredients:
5 cups coleslaw mix with red cabbage and carrots
1/2 cup Vegan Mayo  (or use store bought vegan mayo )
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon celery seed

Instructions:
Add the vegan mayo, granulated sugar, apple cider vinegar, salt and celery seed to a large mixing bowl and whisk together until fully combined.
Add in the coleslaw mix and stir well. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving, if possible.
