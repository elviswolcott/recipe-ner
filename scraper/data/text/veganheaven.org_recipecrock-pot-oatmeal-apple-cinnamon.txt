Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
2 cups oat or almond milk milk
4 tablespoons unsweetened apple sauce
1  apple, cut into medium-sized chunks
1 tablespoon raisins
5  dates, finely chopped
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 cup quinoa
1/4 cup walnuts, chopped

Instructions:
Put all ingredients except for the walnuts in a slow cooker. Cook on low for 9 hours.
Serve warm with some fresh apple chunks and chopped walnuts.
