Ingredients:
1 batch Smoky Baked Tempeh Bacon
1 batch Vegan Mayo (or store bought vegan mayo)
8-12 slices bread of choice (I used sourdough) (*check ingredients to ensure vegan)
1-2 heads lettuce (use either romaine, iceberg or even baby kale for a change)
3-4  large ripe tomatoes, sliced
OPTIONAL: avocado slices and sprouts

Instructions:
Make the tempeh bacon. Make the vegan mayo&nbsp;if using.
Toast your bread, and layer with vegan mayo, tempeh bacon, lettuce, tomato and optional avocado and sprouts.
Serve and enjoy!
