Ingredients:
2 cups buckwheat soba ((cooked acc to instructions and chilled with ice cubes))
Assorted veggies grilled in oil on high flame ((carrots, bell pepper, red pepper, onion, ginger))
cilantro ((grated ginger or other herbs for garnish))
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon rice vinegar
1 teaspoon wasabi ( (we got some fresh from asian store))
1 teaspoon lime juice
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 teaspoon brown sugar

Instructions:
Prepare the soba noodles and chill.
Grill some veggies and add them to the chilled noodles.
Mix in all the dressing ingredients and half teaspoon wasabi well and keep ready.
Serve chilled noodles with dressing on the side and half teaspoon wasabi on top the noodles!
