Ingredients:
1/2  medium cauliflower
2  medium white potatoes
1  Serrano chili pepper
1 inch ginger chopped
3  garlic cloves chopped
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon coriander seeds
1/2 cup cilantro leaves chopped
1/2 teaspoon turmeric ((haldi))
pinch of asafetida ((hing))
salt to taste ((1/2 teaspoon))
2 teaspoons canola oil

Instructions:
In a pan, add oil and heat it at medium heat for 2 minutes.
Add in cumin seeds and wait until they start sputtering.
Add in the asafetida(hing), coriander seeds and turmeric and mix well.
Add in the chopped ginger, garlic, Serrano chili and chopped cilantro and let them cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add in the potatoes chopped into small pieces and mix well and cook for 5 minutes stirring occasionally.
Break the cauliflower into florets or chop some of the bigger florets to form smaller pieces.
Add in the cauliflower florets and salt and mix well.
Reduce the heat to low and cook covered for 15-20 minutes stirring every 5-7 minutes.
Check if potatoes are done and take off heat.
Serve hot with Rotis or Naan(Indian flat bread) and with some Daal(Lentil curry).
