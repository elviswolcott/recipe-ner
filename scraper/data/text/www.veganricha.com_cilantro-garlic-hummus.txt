Ingredients:
15  oz can garbanzo beans  (, washed and drained)
5 cloves of garlic
1  dried red chili pepper or 1 teaspoon chili flakes
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon tahini sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon lime juice
1/4 teaspoon kala namak
1 cup chopped cilantro
3 teaspoons olive oil
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions:
In a pan, heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil.
Add the red chili pepper and the garlic cloves to it.
Cook the garlic on medium high heat for 5 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add in the garbanzo beans and the spices and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
Cool the mixture and blend with cilantro leaves and a little water.
Sprinkle the remaining olive oil and cayenne pepper before serving!
