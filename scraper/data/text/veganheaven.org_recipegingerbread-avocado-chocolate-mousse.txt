Ingredients:
2  ripe avocados
1 large banana
1/2 cup cacao powder
6  dates
1/2 cup almond milk or other plant-based milk
1 teaspoon gingerbread spice
coconut cream
fresh berries
dark chocolate
cinnamon

Instructions:
Put all ingredients into a food processor and process until smooth.
Serve it together with coconut cream and fresh berries and sprinkle it with dark chocolate and cinnamon.
