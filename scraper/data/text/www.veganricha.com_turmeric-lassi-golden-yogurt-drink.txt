Ingredients:
1 cup plain non dairy yogurt
1 cup almond milk or coconut milk frozen into cubes (1 heaping cup cubes)
1 tsp or more turmeric
a good dash of black pepper
a good pinch of cayenne
a pinch of salt (optional)
1 tbsp or more sweetener (sugar, maple, coconut sugar etc)
1 tsp candied ginger  (or 1/2 tsp fresh peeled ginger)

Instructions:
Add everything to the blender, Blend for a minute until smooth. Taste and adjust sweet and blend for 15 seconds. Pour into serving glasses. Add ice cubes if needed.
Add a pinch of turmeric and pepper to garnish or top with some whipped coconut cream. Serve immediately.
