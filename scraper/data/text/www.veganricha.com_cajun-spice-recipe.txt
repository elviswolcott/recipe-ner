Ingredients:
2 tbsp paprika (, sweet + smoked, use more of one or the other based on heat preference. Smoky paprika is generally hotter. )
1/2 - 3/4 tsp cayenne
1.5 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1.5 tsp red pepper flakes
1.5 tsp dried thyme
1 tbsp dried oregano
1 tbsp garlic powder
1.5 tsp onion powder
1 tsp salt ((optional))
optional add ins for variation: (crushed bay leaf, 1/4 tsp ground mustard)

Instructions:
Mix all the ingredients and store in an airtight container for upto 3 months&nbsp;
