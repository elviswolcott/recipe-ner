Ingredients:
1.5 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup boiled lentils (i used yellow mung bean as it cooks quickly)
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp chilli flakes
1/2 tsp garlic flakes
2 tsp oil
2 tsp melted butter
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup water

Instructions:
Boil lentils and cool.
Mash the lentils and add salt, chili and garlic flakes
Sift the wheat flour and baking powder.
Make dough of flour, oil, lentil mix and water.
Let it stand for 10 mins and knead again.
Make a loaf and bake on baking sheet for 45 minutes at 350 degrees F / 180c.
Brush melted butter on top to keep it soft.
Some flaxseed can be sprinkled on the top and also added in the dough for some extra texture.
